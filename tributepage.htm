Jacob Oulanyah

A man of the people

Jacob Oulanyah chairs a parliamentary session

Here is a fact file on Hon.Jacob Oulanyah:

  • 1965 - Born in the then Gulu District,then attends St. Joseph's College Layibi, Dr. Obote College Boroboro, and Kololo Senior Secondary School for his O-Level and A-Level education.
  • 1988 - Joined Makerere University where he studied agricultural economics.
  • 1991 - Graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Agricultural Economics and entered Law School the same year.
  • 1994 - Graduated with a Bachelor of Laws degree from Makerere University.
  • 1995 - obtained a Postgraduate Diploma in Legal Practice from Law Development Centre (LDC) and works at centre as a lecturer. He also starts Oulanyah, Onoria & Company Advocates,a private law practice.
  • 2001 - Successfully contested for the parliamentary seat of Omoro County as a member of Uganda People's Congress (UPC).
  • 2006 - Loses his election bid as a Uganda People's Congress (UPC) candidate then quits the UPC and joined the ruling National Resistance Movement (NRM).
  • 2008 - served as the chairman of the commission of inquiry into the controversial sub-lease of Kisekka Market.
  • 2011 - Oulanyah was elected to represent Omoro County on the National Resistance Movement (NRM) ticket. He was elected as Deputy Speaker of Parliament on 19 May 2011.
  • 2016 - Oulanyah was re-elected as Deputy Speaker of Parliament. On 13 July 2019, he was awarded an honorary Doctorate of Divinity by Zoe Life Theological College USA and acquired the title, Doctor enabling him to be called Rt. Hon. Dr. Jacob L. Oulanyah.
  • 2021 - Jacob Oulanyah was voted Speaker of Parliament. Shortly after his election as Speaker, he reportedly contracted COVID-19 and was flown to the United Kingdom for treatment. He last presided over Parliament on 21 December 2021.
  • 2022 - He passes on in Seattle on March 22 2022.
